Earning Trust Points

Trust is earned by consuming gold in various growth activities:

  • Gear honing (normal/advanced)
    1:1 (normal gold)
  • Quality upgrade
    1:1 (normal gold)
  • Accessory refinement
    1:1 (normal gold)
  • Elixir crafting/refining
    1:1 (normal gold)
  • Karma unlock, level/rank growth
    1:1 (normal gold)
  • Transcendence unlock/purify
    1:1 (normal gold)

1 normal gold used = +1 trust | 1 Roster bound gold used = +0.1 trust | 1 Character bound gold used = +0.1 trust

Trust Deductions

Trust decreases when trading gold with other players:

Action Trust Deduction Additional Fee if Untrusted
Gold-attached mail Equal to gold sent 25% of missing trust
Payment request Equal to gold requested 25% of missing trust
Personal gold transfer Equal to gold sent 25% of missing trust

Example: With 1,000 Trust, when sending 5,000 gold, 4,000 gold without Trust will have an additional 1,000 gold fee (25% of 4,000).
